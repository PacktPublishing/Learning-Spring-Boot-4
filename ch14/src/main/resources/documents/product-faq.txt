=== TechStore Product FAQ ===

Q: What is the return policy?
A: Customers may return any item within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
   Items must be in their original packaging and unused condition.
   Digital downloads are non-refundable once accessed.

Q: What are the shipping options?
A: We offer three shipping tiers:
   - Standard (5-7 business days): free on orders over $50, otherwise $4.99.
   - Express (2-3 business days): $9.99.
   - Overnight (next business day): $24.99.
   Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship the same day.

Q: What is the warranty on the SpringBook Pro?
A: The SpringBook Pro carries a 2-year limited hardware warranty covering manufacturing
   defects. Physical damage and liquid damage are not covered. An extended TechCare plan
   is available at $49/year and covers accidental damage, including one screen replacement
   per year and unlimited battery replacements.

Q: Is the SpringBook Pro compatible with Java 25?
A: Yes. The SpringBook Pro ships with JDK 25 LTS pre-installed and is fully certified for
   Spring Boot 4 workloads. The integrated NPU (Neural Processing Unit) accelerates local
   AI inference using Ollama at up to 40 tokens per second for 7B-parameter models.

Q: What are the technical specifications of the SpringBook Pro?
A: Processor: 12-core ARM CPU (4 performance + 8 efficiency cores).
   Memory: 16 GB unified RAM (32 GB optional upgrade).
   Storage: 512 GB NVMe SSD (1 TB and 2 TB options available).
   Display: 14-inch OLED, 2880x1800, 120 Hz ProMotion.
   Battery: Up to 18 hours of typical use.
   Ports: 2x USB-C Thunderbolt 5, 1x HDMI 2.1, 1x SD card reader, 1x 3.5mm audio.

Q: How do I contact customer support?

Q: Does TechStore offer student or educational discounts?
A: Yes. Students and educators with a valid .edu email address receive 15% off all hardware.
   Educational institutions purchasing 10 or more units qualify for volume pricing.
   Contact sales@techstore.example.com for a custom quote.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and
   TechStore gift cards. Buy-now-pay-later is available through Affirm for orders over $200.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Once your order ships you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can also
   log into your account at techstore.example.com/orders to view real-time shipping status.
   Estimated delivery dates are updated automatically as the package moves through the carrier.
